Welcome to Health For Everyone

The Program, Health for Everyone - للجمیع الصحة is a joint venture of Dubai Ministry of Health, residents, doctors and business personnels of Dubai.

This is an effort towards building a healthier community by helping those who cannot afford to consult a General Physician for common illnesses, hence turning them into prolonged, complex diseases and further spreading the germs to other members of the community. In UAE, technicians are paid 7,000 AED/month while laborers get 5,000 a month. This program targets all those who fall into this salary bracket (7,000 AED and below/month).

  • This program is mainly funded by the Government, residents, pharmacies and doctors of UAE in terms of monetary donations as well as services.
  • Interactive Kiosks are provided at various locations, which are easily accessible by all. These Kiosks not only provide the chance of consulting a doctor for free but also help donors to extend a helping hand by donating as much as they wish to. Every kiosk has a height and weight measuring device, in order to help the patient, provide correct information to the doctor.
  • Video Consultation with a doctor gives a sense of visiting one in person and gives a much better experience to the patient, as well as, giving liberty to the doctor to help the patient from wherever he is.
  • Patient’s personal and consultation information is saved prior to the consultation, hence saving the time of doctor, leaving him with more time to help other underprivileged patients.
  • Illnesses are nipped in the bud! Patients are treated at the initial stage of sickness, which means recovering in a much shorter duration with the help of least or no medication.
  • Since the medication is free and recommended by a doctor, the patient isn’t hesitant to treat himself with those.
  • Patient’s medical history and prescriptions are saved in the system, hence letting the medical professional have a look at the previous medications that the patient has taken.
  • Since it is a UAE Ministry of Health initiative, there’s no fear of it being fraudulent or misused. Data provided by doctors, pharmacies and patients remain 100% confidential.

We are building a better Dubai for our kids. Would you help us? Please donate today!
